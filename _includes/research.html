Rocket Tracking Camera

For investigations of rocket flights, usually flight data such as from an accelerometer is up to interpretation. While on-board cameras allow for a specific view point they are often limited by; field of view, structural requirements and avionics requirements. If the flight results in a catastrophic failure, data may be unrecoverable.

My research will explore a ground tracking system a Camera or Antenna that can track the rocket during flight allowing for a stable down-link of data and appropriate video footage, improving the quality of data for post-flight investigations. Control may be provided by a human in the loop, or completely automated.
